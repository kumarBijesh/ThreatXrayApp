ThreatXray is an advanced web application designed to enhance digital security by providing powerful scanning and analysis tools. Our platform empowers users to:

• Scan Files for Threats :– Upload and analyze files to detect potential security risks, such as malware or other malicious content.
• Scan URLs for Risks :– Check website links for phishing attempts, malware, or unsafe content before accessing them.
• Analyze Photo Metadata :– Extract and review metadata from uploaded images, including GPS location, camera details, and other embedded information.
• User-Controlled Metadata Removal :– After scanning, users can decide whether to keep or remove metadata for enhanced privacy.

ThreatXray aims to provide a secure and user-friendly experience, helping individuals and organizations safeguard their data against potential cyber threats.
